Tricity Swim's Secret Formula

Focused private lessons

Personal coaching. Better progress.

At TriCity Swim, we believe swim lessons should feel focused, encouraging, and worth every dollar. That is why we put so much value on one-on-one coaching at an affordable price.

In many group lessons, swimmers spend a lot of time waiting for their turn. With fewer turns and less actual time moving through the water, progress can be slower. A swimmer may be present for the full lesson, but the amount of real swimming practice is often limited.

Private lessons change that. Our swimmers get more direct feedback, more repetition, more attention to technique, and more time actually practicing key skills in the water. That means stronger progress, better confidence, and lessons that feel more personal from start to finish.

We teach by the Lifesaving Society swim curriculum. Swimmers learn skills step by step as they become safer and more confident in the water. They work on basics such as entering and exiting the pool safely, submerging, floating, gliding, kicking, and breathing. As they improve, they learn stroke development like front crawl, back crawl, and whip kick, along with treading water, swimming longer distances, and deep-water confidence. The curriculum also teaches important water safety skills, such as how to stay calm, make smart choices around water, and respond safely in different situations.
